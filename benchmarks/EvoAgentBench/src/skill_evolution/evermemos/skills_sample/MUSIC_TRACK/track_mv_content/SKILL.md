# Music Track: MV Content Search

## When to use
When a music track question describes the visual content of a music video or its cultural significance.

## Technique
MV content descriptions appear in review articles and video descriptions, making them highly searchable. Approach:
1. Extract MV content keywords from the question: "grief", "mother and child", "ferry tragedy", "loss", specific visual scenes.
2. Search `"music video [content description] [emotional keywords]"`.
3. If the MV references a real-world event (Sewol ferry tragedy, historical event), add that event name to the search.
4. MV descriptions in articles often mention the track name and artist — extract both from results.

Also consider: "last music video before disbanding" is a distinctive search phrase. Combined with MV content, it can directly locate the track.

## Query Templates
- `"music video [content description] [emotional keywords] [event name]"`
- `""last music video" before disbanding [MV content description]"`
- `"[track name] music video meaning symbolism [event]"`

## Worked Examples
**The Light:** Question described a group's last MV depicting a mother and child's grief, related to a ferry disaster. Searched `"music video mother child grief loss tragedy ferry disaster"` → found The Ark's "The Light" referencing the Sewol ferry tragedy.

**Abangan 2024:** Question described a song adapted into a musical. Searched `"musical play based on song premiered late 2010s final show 2023"` → found Ang Huling El Bimbo musical → traced back to the original track.

## Anti-pattern
Not searching for MV content at all. When a question describes what happens in the music video, those visual descriptions are powerful search anchors — add "music video [content]" to your queries.
