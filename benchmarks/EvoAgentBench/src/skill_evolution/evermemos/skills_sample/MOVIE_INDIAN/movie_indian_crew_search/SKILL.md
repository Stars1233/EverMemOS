# Crew Role Search

## When to use
When the question asks about a specific crew member (production controller, costume designer, music director, choreographer) of a South Asian film — not just director or lead actors.

## Technique
Indian film questions often require identifying specific crew members beyond director and lead actors. These include production controller, costume designer, music director, art director, and choreographer.

First identify the target movie through other clues (plot, director, actors). Then search for the specific crew role using the movie's cast and crew page. Wikipedia, IMDb, and regional film databases list these roles.

Search directly with `"[movie name]" [crew role]` or `"[movie name]" cast and crew` to find the detailed credits page.

## Query Templates
- `"[movie name]" production controller`
- `"[movie name]" cast and crew credits`
- `"[movie name]" costume designer music director`

## Worked Examples

### Example
- Question: Who was the production controller of a certain Indian movie whose director also made another specific film?
- First located the Malayalam movie Pachakuthira through plot clues
- Then searched: `Pachakuthira production controller producer Babu Shahir`
- Why it worked: Direct crew role search on the identified movie found the answer immediately

## Anti-pattern
- **Neglecting crew searches**: Assuming the answer is always a director or actor. Indian film questions frequently ask about specialized crew roles that require dedicated credit page searches.
